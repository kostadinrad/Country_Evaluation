Regional Overview

Europe remains the world's second-largest gambling market by absolute size, with USD 24B in total addressable market across 30+ jurisdictions. However, the region is transitioning through a critical inflection point in 2026. Major regulatory events include the Netherlands' critical license renewal (October 1 deadline), Italy's landmark 9-year tender, and widespread implementation of stricter responsible gambling measures. The regulatory environment is tightening but remains more stable than emerging markets. 2026 Strategic Pivot: Europe is shifting from growth focus to consolidation and compliance. Entry barriers are rising due to AML/KYC intensification and mandatory technical certifications (ISO standards). Existing operators in good regulatory standing have competitive advantage; new entrants face higher friction.
